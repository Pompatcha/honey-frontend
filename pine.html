Pine Honey

Total: 250 THB

Description:

Pine honey is a unique type of honey made from the sap of pine trees, not flower nectar. It has a deep amber color and rich, slightly woody taste. Known for its antioxidant properties and mineral richness, pine honey is a nutritious sweetener perfect for teas, toast, or spoonful enjoyment.
